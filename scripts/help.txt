Steps to use the application

1- Click on "Genetic Data" and then on "File Browser" to add a genetic file

2- Click on "Sequence Alignment" to create the alignment. This step may take a few minutes. Alternatively,
you can do step 3 before step 2

3- Click on "Climatic Data" and then on "File Browser" to add a climatic file

4- Click on "Statistics" to show the climatic bar graph

5- Click on "Results" and then on "Submit" to filter the data according to a specific threshold.
You can change the filter in "Settings"

You will obtain more information here : https://github.com/tahiri-lab/aPhyloGeo_plus_plus/blob/main/README.md
